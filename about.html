Our Story

Cơm Tấm THANH (Pronounced Kom Tham Tainh) is a family-owned business that was established back in 1998, nearly 20 years ago. Since then we have expanded to a total of three locations within the Bay Area. However, business was not always booming; the business had a rough start at first. In the beginning, there was only a few workers…..

Thus, we are truly grateful to our customers. We strive to provide quality authentic Vietnamese cuisine and with our extensive menu, we hope to be able to offer dishes of many varieties to suit everyone's tastes. In addition to the popular Vietnamese beef soup, we also serve Cơm tấm or steamed broken rice with various types of grilled pork or beef and Nước chấm or sweet and sour dipping sauce. Unlike other Vietnamese restaurants, we have other unique delicious dishes, like Bún Bò Huế, Bánh Cuốn and caramel River Bard.
