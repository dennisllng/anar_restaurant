OUR STORY

Cơm Tấm THANH (Pronounced Kom Tham Tainh) is a family-owned business that was opened back in 2010. Since then, we have expanded and now have three locations. We strive to provide authentic Vietnamese cuisine and with our extensive menu, we hope to be able to offer dishes of many varieties to suit everyone's tastes. In addition to the popular Vietnamese beef soup, we also serve Cơm tấm or steamed broken rice with various types of grilled pork or beef and Nước chấm or sweet and sour dipping sauce. Unlike other Vietnamese restaurants, we have other unique delicious dishes, like Bún Bò Huế, Bánh Cuốn and caramel River Bard.
